About Us

The Maintenance Training Organization under AIESL is a DGCA approved organisation, under CAR 147. The MTO has developed facilities to train personnel of the Engineering Department and conducts training programs. Apart from training the engineering personnel of AIESL, MTO conducts training programs for Air India Limited, Air-India Express Limited, Air India Regional and customized aviation maintenance training for other agencies like Civil Aviation Department of Govt. of India,Indian Air Force, Indian Navy, vendors, other Airlines, AME Training Schools and other State and Central Govt Organisations..
